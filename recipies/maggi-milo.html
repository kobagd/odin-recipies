Maggi Milo

Description

This is a recipie for the most experimental version of instant noodles known to mankind. You either love or hate it - there is no in-between.

Please contact your nearest medical practitioner should you experience any forms of indigestion, especially during the cooking process.

Ingredients

  • Milo powder
  • Maggi mee packet

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. Remove maggi mee from the packet and put it in the boiling water.
  3. Cook the maggi mee for 3 minutes until it's springy.
  4. Stop boiling the water and strain the cooked maggi mee, but leave some water as the soup.
  5. Add 1-2 tablespoons of milo powder and mix it all together.
  6. Question your life choices.
